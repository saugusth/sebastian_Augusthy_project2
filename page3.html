My Top 5 Animated Movies

Number 5: Planet Hulk

After the Hulk becomes too much for the Avengers to handle, he is sent into space. He lands on a planet ruled by the red king and is forced to be a gladiator. With the help of new frends, can he free himself of slavery? This is a good movie for people who want to see Hulk fight with both his fist and his brain.

Number 4: Next Avengers: Heroes of Tomorrow

This movie takes place in the future. With the loss of their parents (The Avengers), The kids James Rogers (son of Captain America and Black Widow), Torunn (daughter of Thor and Sif), Henry Pym Jr. (son of Giant Man and Wasp), and Azari (son of Black Panther and Storm) and Francis Barton (son of Hawkeye and Mockingbird) must help Tony Stark take out Ultron. This movie is a great movie as it shows how even children can be the mightest hereos.

Number 3: Batman Beyond: Return of the Joker

This takes place in the future where a new Batman has to stop joker who was assumed to be dead along with his gang the Jokerz. As the movie goes on, viewers will learn the real truth behind the Joker.This is a great movie for people who would like to learn about old Batman and his past.

Number 2: Teen Titans: Trouble in Tokyo

After fighting with a villan named Saiko-Tek, the teen titans find themselves going to Tokyo where they find themselves in battle with an ink evil. This is a great movie for people who would like to see the Teen Titans in a new environment.

Number 1: Justice League: The Flashpoint Paradox

After being tricked into messing up the timeline, Bary Allen a.k.a The Flash must navigate the the new timeline where Superman does not exist. Bruce dies in the alley and Wonder Women and Aquaman are about to start WW3. Can Flash save the day? This is a great movie for fans who want to see Flash in a brand new enviroment struggling to save the day while keeping himself sane.
